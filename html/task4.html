Angela's Recipie for the Best cinnamon rolls

Ingredients

For the dough

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granualated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 table spoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 Cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the list with the yeast , sugar, eggs.
  2. melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. add in the floor and mix until combined into the dough.
  4. knead the dough for 10 min
  5. transfer the dough into large bowl and cover with the plastic wrap. Leaveit some where to rise for 2 hours
  6. After the bough has doubled the size roll it into rectangles
